Hawks Logo Game








You will be given a random "number to guess" at the start of the game.

There are four Hawks logos below. By clicking on a logo you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the "number to guess". You lose the game if your total score goes above he random number.

The value of each logo is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the values on the logos change too!
